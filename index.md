<section class="intro">
  <h2>What is HelixNote?</h2>
  <p>HelixNote is a professional-grade documentation system tailored for individuals tracking complex medical conditions, interdisciplinary research, or longitudinal data. Designed for clarity, structure, and longevity, HelixNote makes it easy to create meaningful, reference-ready reports with embedded citations and symptom mapping tools.</p>
</section>

<section class="features">
  <h2>Key Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Precision Logging:</strong> Capture timestamped notes, lab results, and structured summaries.</li>
    <li><strong>Symptom Mapping:</strong> Cross-reference symptoms with likely diagnoses in a tabular, searchable format.</li>
    <li><strong>Medical Report Builder:</strong> Create clean, physician-ready summaries with AMA-style citations and a bibliography.</li>
    <li><strong>Private & Secure:</strong> HelixNote is a secure, cloud-hosted application. Your data is encrypted, accessible only to you, and never shared without your consent.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="case-study">
  <h2>Case Study</h2>
  <p>When one family faced an undiagnosed chronic illness, HelixNote enabled them to maintain a timeline of symptoms, lab data, medical history, and physician interpretations. This allowed multiple specialists to stay aligned and contributed to a more comprehensive diagnostic effort.</p>
</section>
