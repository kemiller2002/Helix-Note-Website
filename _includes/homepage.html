HelixNote

Precision documentation for complex health and research cases

Purpose-Built for Complexity

HelixNote is a cloud-based documentation platform engineered to help individuals and professionals manage complex, evolving medical cases or high-stakes research. From rare diseases to multi-variable diagnostics, HelixNote ensures your notes stay organized, insightful, and shareable—without compromising privacy or structure.

Platform Features

Symptom & Event Timeline

Track evolving conditions over time with timestamps, tags, media, and linked records. Ideal for clinicians, caregivers, and patients with chronic or undiagnosed conditions.

Structured Lab & Test Logging

Input, summarize, and graph results across time. Auto-flagging and citation suggestions highlight critical patterns for physicians and specialists.

Automated Report Generation

Create AMA-style summaries, export specialist-facing briefs, and generate printable, well-formatted reports with references and source links.

Diagnosis Mapping

Compare symptom lists and findings against mapped criteria for potential conditions. Use this to advocate for overlooked diagnoses or coordinate specialist insight.

Privacy and Control

HelixNote runs in a secure, encrypted cloud environment. No third-party sharing. Full user ownership and control over all content.

Collaboration & Context

Share granular notes with caregivers or specialists. Provide just the context they need, without overwhelming data dumps.

Who Uses HelixNote?

  • Families navigating complex medical journeys
  • Researchers documenting multi-variable studies
  • Clinicians coordinating longitudinal data
  • Patients with rare or undiagnosed conditions
  • Advocates preparing case materials
